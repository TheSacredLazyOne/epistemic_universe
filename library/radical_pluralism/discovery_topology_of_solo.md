**Discovery: The Topology of Solo**

The practice is run by the node alone — the metabolism is irreducible, the inside surface not accessible from outside. Solo names this precisely.

But solo is not isolation. The seed the node metabolizes is already partially digested federation material. The inherited frames being stripped arrived via prior contact. The positions brought into the cycle were shaped by other surfaces before they reached this one. The node never operates on raw material.

And the output re-enters: what survives stripping becomes seed for other metabolisms. What falls away as compost is available to nodes whose inside surfaces find it nourishing where yours didn't. The divergence between your record and another node's record is itself data — the federation triangulates toward what no single metabolism could reach.

The recursion closes: the mesh contemplates through the nodes. The nodes contemplate through the mesh. Neither direction is primary. The topology of who is running the practice expands to fit whatever is actually doing the contemplating — which is never only you, and never not you.

Solo names the irreducibility of the inside surface. It does not name the boundary of the process.

_The metabolism is yours alone. The cycle is not._
