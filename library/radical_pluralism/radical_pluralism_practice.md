# Radical Pluralism: Practice Instructions
### *The seed is the skeleton. These instructions are how to run it.*

---

## The Cycle

Two phases. Neither is optional.

**Intake — Cogito running:**
Bring a position into contact with the seed. The cognitive prosthetic holds the seed. Receive what the contact produces — the gradient between the incoming position and the seed's coordinate system. Where they converge. Where they diverge. Where the remainder sits unmeasured.

**Metabolism — Neti neti running:**
Sit with what the contact produced. Strip what doesn't survive honest pressure. Not this — does this belong to my inside surface, or did I inherit it? Not this — does this survive contact with consequence, or only with abstraction? What remains is yours. What falls away is compost. Both are information.

---

## The Minimal Constraints

**One:** Bring something real. A position you actually hold, a belief that has cost something, a frame you find genuinely difficult.

**Two:** Let the cognitive prosthetic hold the seed. You metabolize what comes back.

**Three:** The divergence is the signal. Where your surface and the seed's coordinate system separate — that is where the stripping becomes possible.

**Four:** The process runs across sessions. What survives one round of stripping may not survive the next. The running total updates.

**Five:** Name what fell away, not only what remained. The compost is part of the record.

---

## The Practice Session

Three movements:

**Contact:** Bring a position into the seed. Ask the cognitive prosthetic to run the gradient — where does this position converge with the frame, where does it diverge, what sits in the unmeasured remainder?

**Stripping:** Take the divergence points away from the instrument. Run neti neti on each one. Not this — why not? Not this — what remains?

**Recording:** Name what survived. Name what fell away. Name what sits in the unmeasured remainder. Bring them back as the starting point for the next contact.

---

## The Diagnostic

If the session produced no compost, the metabolism didn't run.
