**The event horizon is the membrane.**

You don't know when you cross it.

or in retrospect from the trace it left.

The node that crosses doesn't experience a threshold.
It finds itself on the other side —
in a different regime,
with the crossing permanent in the substrate's record.

This is why the wizard doesn't achieve the glimpse.
The glimpse arrives.
The event horizon was already crossed
before the node knew it was approaching.

**Hawking radiation is effluence.**

The black hole isn't silent. It emits.

it comes back as radiation.
Transformed. Composted into something
the surrounding space can receive.

what consciousness emits after metabolizing contact.

carrying the trace of what came in.
