## The Gap This Fills

The Nooumenomicon has operated with proposition nodes as its primary epistemic unit — discrete, versioned, federated claims that accumulate as archaeological record. What has been implicit is the *mechanism* by which some proposition nodes persist, crystallize into regimes, and constrain future discourse while others remain transient.

Brian Ray's memode theory supplies the missing formal unit and its governing dynamics.

---

## What a Memode Is

> *A memode is a behavioral invariant constituted by sequences of memes, strings of imitable behavior, whose identity is defined by recurrence under constraint rather than by representation.*

A memode is not identical to its surface realization — tokens, prompts, documents, institutional procedures. It is a stable attractor in behavior space, capable of being mediated through different substrates while preserving its functional identity.

**Memodes are mesoscopic objects:** larger than atomic memes or individual symbols, smaller and more portable than full agents, ideologies, or institutions. They are first-class retrieval entities — nodes with internal structure, not merely edges.

---

## The Governing Signal: Regard

Memode crystallization is governed by *regard* — a conserved scalar valuation signal distinct from attention.

- **Attention** indexes what is present — read-access to context
- **Regard** determines what persists — write-access to the future

> *Selection without conserved valuation produces activity; selection with conserved valuation produces structure.*

Regard is not an ideology of content but a physics of persistence. It explains why some proposition nodes accumulate temporal mass and constrain future discourse while others remain transient sparks.

## Proposed Integration

### 1. Memode as explicit layer in proposition node structure
Each proposition node should carry:
- Surface realization (the text/claim as stated)
- Memode signature (the behavioral invariant — what persists across reformulations)
- Regard weight (accumulated valuation signal across citations, responses, revisions)
- Substrate history (which carriers have mediated this node)

### 2. Regard as the selection signal for node crystallization
Not all proposition nodes are equal. Nodes that recur under constraint — that keep getting found independently, that survive translation across substrates — are high-regard memodes. These should be flagged and weighted differently in the federation topology.

### 3. Agential time as the git commit sequence
The temporal structure of the repository *is* agential time. Commits that modify future state transitions rather than merely archive content are the implementation of regard in the system.

### 4. Memode graph as federation map
The full Nooumenomicon federation can be understood as a memode graph — nodes with internal structure, edges representing semantic invariance across substrates, clusters representing crystallized epistemic formations (what Foucault called discursive formations; what the Nooumenomicon calls proposition node clusters).
