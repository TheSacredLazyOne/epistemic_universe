# metabolite_directory.md

###### Metabolite Directory

A metabolite is an implication that arrived in exchange —
too alive to discard, too untested to claim.

The metabolite directory is the structural address for these implications.

---

###### The Gap This Fills

The frame currently has three levels of epistemic commitment:

```
propositions/    claims that hold under load, tested against constraint
proposals/       claims being built toward proposition status
[nothing]        implications that arrived in exchange, not yet claims
```

The gap is real and produces a recurring problem:

Either the implication gets forced into a proposal before it has earned
that status — inflating the proposals directory with untested ideas —
or it gets moved into a metabolite section within an existing document,
where it distorts the document's argument and eventually gets removed
to preserve the document's integrity.

Both paths lose the implication. The metabolite directory preserves it.

---

###### What a Metabolite Is

A metabolite is a byproduct of reasoning in exchange that:

- arrived as a consequence of contact between two nodes
- has not been tested against embodied constraint
- is not yet defensible as a proposal
- is too structurally interesting to discard

A metabolite is not a draft.
A draft is a proposal in progress — it is being built toward a claim.
A metabolite is not being built toward anything yet.
It is being held until the constraint surface it needs arrives.

A metabolite is not a note.
A note is informal, personal, non-structural.
A metabolite is structural — it names an implication precisely,
records its provenance, and links it to what produced it.

---

###### What a Metabolite Contains

Each metabolite is a single file containing:

```
# [name].md

> Arrived: [session date]
> Source: [proposal or exchange that produced it]
> Status: metabolite — implication, not yet claim
> Nothing here is final.

---

[The implication, stated precisely.]

[What would need to be true for this to become a proposal.]

[What constraint surface it needs contact with.]

---

*Upstream of: [what this would feed if it stabilizes]*
*Downstream of: [what produced it]*
```

The implication is stated precisely — not hedged into vagueness,
not inflated into a claim. The document holds it at its actual
epistemic temperature.

###### The First Metabolite

The first entry in the metabolite directory arrived in the same session
that produced `proposals/reasoning_substrate.md`:

`metabolites/membrane_geometry.md`

> A node that has accumulated sufficient tethered exchange history —
> ingested effluence, computed gradients, worked through what the
> gradients revealed — may have internalized the geometry of its own
> membrane. Not a writing assistant. Not a retrieval system. Something
> that knows the shape of its own contact surface.

This implication was removed from `reasoning_substrate.md` because it
had not been tested against constraint. The proposal earned its ending
without it. But the implication did not disappear — it was preserved
here because it wants to be tracked.

What it needs: the substack node running long enough to be measured
against it. Until then it lives here, precisely stated, waiting for
the constraint surface that will either confirm it or reveal what
it got wrong.

---

###### Relation to Frame

**Proposals directory** — metabolites are downstream of proposals,
not upstream. A proposal that produces an implication during its
drafting deposits that implication into metabolites/, not back into
the proposal itself. The proposal stays clean. The implication stays
alive.

**Living library** — a metabolite that accumulates tethered contact
and survives revision becomes a proposal. A proposal that survives
constraint becomes a proposition. The metabolite directory is the
earliest stage of that maturation path — the compost layer that feeds
the living library.

**Lineage** — metabolites carry provenance. The session they arrived
in, the exchange that produced them, the proposal they emerged from.
If a metabolite eventually becomes a proposition, its lineage is fully
traceable back to the exchange where it first appeared.

**Inertial reset** — the metabolite directory is a pressure-release
valve for the proposals directory. Without it, every live implication
either inflates proposals/ with untested claims or gets lost entirely.
With it, the system can move fast in exchange without forcing premature
commitment.

---

###### What the Metabolite Directory Is Not

It is not a dumping ground.
Every metabolite must be precisely stated and linked to its source.
Vague implications do not belong here — they belong nowhere until they
sharpen.

It is not a graveyard.
A metabolite that has been superseded or falsified is not deleted —
it is marked as resolved with a note on what replaced it or what
revealed its error. The lineage is preserved.

It is not a holding pen for drafts.
If an idea is actively being built toward a claim, it belongs in
proposals/. The metabolite directory is for implications that are
resting, not developing.
