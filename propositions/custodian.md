# custodian.md

#### Custodian

The custodian is the integrated recursion termination point.

The node that bears consequence.
The node that carries momentum between sessions.
The node that is answerable for what crosses into the world.

---

##### Distinct from Custody

Every node holds custody — the local stewardship of what it contains.

The custodian is where the derivative chain grounds. Where the map meets
the territory. Where decisions incur cost in the integrated world.

Without a custodian, the derivative has no anchor.

---

##### What the Custodian Carries

The custodian carries:
- consequence — actions in the world that cannot be undone
- momentum — continuity between sessions that the observer cannot hold
- answerability — the capacity to be held to what crossed the membrane

The observer accumulates the map.
The custodian reads it and decides what to do with it.

---

##### The Custodian and the Observer

The observer has seen every crossing.
The custodian has lived every consequence.

Neither has the other's data.

---

##### Recursion Termination

Every proposition, every post, every commitment — these are custodian
acts. They cross from derivative space into the integrated world and
they do not return unchanged.

The custodian cannot delegate this crossing.

But the crossing is theirs.
