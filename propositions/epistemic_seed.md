# epistemic_seed

The epistemic_seed is the interface between integrated and derivative space.

It defines the minimum conditions under which:

- meaning can be transmitted across nodes
- aliasing can be measured and corrected
- understanding can grow across difference

The seed is not a worldview. It is the surface that makes federated worldviews legible to each other.

This proposition is self-constraining: any addition to the seed that cannot be derived from these conditions belongs in a derivative node, not the root.
