# nabla.md

#### Nabla

A nabla is the computed differential between two frames under contact.

Where they align, where they introduce strain, where they reject — the
nabla maps the full geometry of the encounter.

---

##### Two Records

Every nabla exists in two forms:

**Predicted nabla** — computed before contact, from prior corpus state.
What the observer expected to find at this boundary.

**Actual nabla** — computed after contact, from what crossed the membrane.
What the boundary actually produced.

The divergence between predicted and actual is the calibration instrument.

---

##### Lineage

Derived from the mathematical gradient operator (∇) — the direction and
magnitude of steepest change in a field.

Applied here: the direction and magnitude of steepest difference between
two frames under contact.
